## Welcome to AOG Lentsweletau

Assemblies of God Lentsweletau is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community of believers in the heart of Lentsweletau Village, Kweneng District, Botswana. Our village name means "Rock of the Lion" in Setswana, and we stand firm on the Rock of our faith — Jesus Christ.

We are part of the Assemblies of God family in Botswana and around the world. Our church is committed to worship, discipleship, fellowship and reaching our community with the love of Christ.

## What We Believe

As part of the Assemblies of God, we hold to the **16 Fundamental Truths** of the Bible. At the heart of our faith are **four cardinal doctrines**:

<div class="beliefs-grid">
  <div class="belief-card">
    <h3>Salvation</h3>
    <p>Salvation through faith in Jesus Christ — the only way to be reconciled with God. By grace through faith, not by works, so that no one may boast.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="belief-card">
    <h3>Baptism in the Holy Spirit</h3>
    <p>The baptism in the Holy Spirit with the initial physical evidence of speaking in other tongues, empowering believers for witness and service.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="belief-card">
    <h3>Divine Healing</h3>
    <p>Divine healing is provided for in the atonement and is the privilege of all believers. Jesus bore our sicknesses and carried our diseases.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="belief-card">
    <h3>Second Coming of Christ</h3>
    <p>The blessed hope — the soon coming return of our Lord Jesus Christ to gather His people and establish His kingdom.</p>
  </div>
</div>

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God. We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons — Father, Son and Holy Spirit. We believe in water baptism by immersion and the Lord's Supper as ordinances of the Church.

For the full statement of faith, visit [ag.org](https://ag.org).

## Part of the AG Family

Assemblies of God Lentsweletau is part of the **Assemblies of God Botswana**, which is part of the worldwide Assemblies of God fellowship — one of the largest Pentecostal denominations in the world with over 69 million adherents.
